M.S. Thesis  ·  Data Engineering & Machine Learning

Arlo Hotels Revenue Analysis & Forecasting System

Engineered an end to end forecasting system on a two years data set that includes reservations and revenue data from Arlo Hotels. Designed a Python & Pandas ETL pipeline to ingest and clean PMS exports, then trained a LightGBM model with feature engineering across day of week, lead time, seasonality, and local event signals achieving R² 0.97 and ~7% MAPE across properties. Deployed a Streamlit dashboard that surfaces live 30/60/90-day occupancy forecasts for revenue managers with zero model-layer exposure.
